Research

I’m broadly interested in what we can learn about the Milky Way and its stars using data from the exciting astrometric space mission Gaia together with on-going spectroscopic surveys such as APOGEE(-2), GALAH and Gaia-ESO. Particularly I’m interested in ways to find, characterize comoving stars, and understand their statistics in Galactic context.
