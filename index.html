Hello, there! I’m Semyeong /se-mjʌŋ/, and I am a PhD student in Astrophysics at Princeton. I’m interested in stars that are associated kinematically or chemically, and what these stars tell us about the Milky Way.

I’m also interested in statistical inference, machine learning, data visualization, and good software practices in general.

You can contact me via e-mail.
